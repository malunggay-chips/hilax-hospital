About Hilax General Hospital

Hilax General Hospital — History

Founded in 1998, Hilax General Hospital began as a small community clinic with only four physicians and limited facilities. Over the years, the hospital expanded into a full-service medical center to meet the growing needs of the region. Today, Hilax General Hospital provides multidisciplinary care, diagnostic imaging, surgical services, and emergency support. The institution continues to modernize its systems, embracing digital record-keeping and patient-centered innovation to ensure faster, safer, and more efficient service delivery.

Mission Statement

To deliver compassionate, quality healthcare to every patient through excellence, integrity, and continuous improvement — ensuring that every individual receives timely and effective medical care.

Vision Statement

To become a leading regional medical center recognized for patient-focused service, advanced technology, and a collaborative healthcare workforce dedicated to saving lives and improving community well-being.
